Storyline

In a forgotten land, deep beneath the mountains, sleeps a dragon. The last of the great ones. His golden treasure lies hidden in his cavern, between fire and shadow, protected by ancient words of power.

To claim his treasure, you must unravel the secret word before the dragon awakens. Each mistake stirs him from his slumber and brings your closer to the flames.

Chose your path, adventurer.

Rules

Classical

Three levels of difficulty - Easy, Medium and Difficult. With each level, words get more longer and complicated. Easy level has free hint while use of hint in Medium and Difficult cost one life.

Timed

You have a limited time to guess the word before Dragon awakens. If time runs out, you loose. Free hint available.

Category

Choose the name to guess from different categories. Free hint available.

Learning

You are given a definition, and must name the word. No hints available.
